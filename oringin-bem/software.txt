Software details

This CD contains Fortran source codes and example data files for 
chapters in Boundary Element Acoustics, edited by Tim Wu. © WIT Press 2000. 
The source codes are universal and can be compiled on any computer 
with a Fortran compiler.

In the folders Chapter3 and Chapter4, PC-executibles complied from the source 
codes are also included. The executibles are ready to run and are for PCs only.

Systems requirements:
32 MB of available RAM
100 MB of available hard-disk space
